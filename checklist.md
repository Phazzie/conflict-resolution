## ⚠️ Red Flags to Watch For

- **User abandonment spike** during AI interactions (fix immediately)
- **Negative feedback** about AI being "mean" or "unhelpful" (pivot tone)
- **Mobile usage dropping** (mobile experience is broken)
- **No professional interest** (credibility problem)
- **High technical metrics, low user satisfaction** (optimization theatre)
